Dr. Pramod Bhatt

Scientific Officer BARC Mumbai

Research Focus

Dr. Bhatt specializes in the Magnetism Section of the Solid State Physics Division. His work focuses on neutron scattering techniques to understand magnetic structures in rare-earth intermetallics. He has been instrumental in the development of advanced shielding materials for nuclear applications.

Conference Role

As the primary Scientific Advisor, Dr. Bhatt oversees the peer-review process for the Magnetism track and coordinates with international keynote speakers.

Dr. M. Deepty

Professor in Physics SITE, Tadepalligudem

Research Focus

Dr. Deepty heads the Nanomaterials & Nanomagnetism Laboratory at Sasi Institute. Her research interests span multiferroic composites, diluted magnetic semiconductors, and spintronics. She actively mentors PhD scholars in advanced material synthesis.

Conference Role

As the Local Organizing Chair, Dr. Deepty manages the onsite logistics, student volunteer teams, and the coordination of the abstract submission portal.
